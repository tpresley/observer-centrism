## Prediction

Unitarity — the preservation of total quantum mechanical probability — is the exact statement of coherence conservation applied to quantum dynamics. It admits **no corrections, no exceptions, no scale at which it fails**. No objective collapse, no nonlinear Schrödinger corrections, no unitarity violation in black hole evaporation.

## Derivation Sketch

1. Coherence conservation (Axiom 1) is absolute
2. Unitarity is coherence conservation expressed in the quantum formalism
3. Therefore unitarity is exact at all scales

## Testability

Precision tests of quantum superposition with increasingly massive objects (matter-wave interferometry with molecules, nanoscale oscillators, future macroscopic tests) probe this directly. The framework predicts no deviation from exact unitary evolution.
