## Statement

**Goal.** Show that the strong CP problem is resolved by the octonionic structure of SU(3) color: the topological $\theta$-term is forbidden, giving $\theta = 0$ exactly.

The derivation should:

1. Show that the octonionic structure of SU(3) constrains the vacuum topology
2. Prove that the $\theta$-parameter of QCD is forced to zero by the non-associative algebra
3. Demonstrate that no axion field is needed (or predicted) to solve the strong CP problem
4. Verify consistency with the observed upper bound $|\theta| < 10^{-10}$

## Dependencies

This derivation requires:
- [Color Force](/derivations/gauge/color-force) — the octonionic origin of SU(3) color symmetry

## Predictive Content

If successful, this derivation yields a distinctive prediction: **no axion particle exists**. Current and future axion searches (ADMX, CASPEr, IAXO, etc.) should find null results. This is in tension with models where axions constitute dark matter.
