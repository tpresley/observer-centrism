## Statement

**Goal.** Prove that the framework's discrete spacetime structure resolves all classical singularities (Big Bang, black hole interior) by providing a natural minimum length scale.

The derivation should:

1. Show that the relational invariant network has a minimum resolution scale set by the Planck length
2. Prove that curvature is bounded: $R_{\mu\nu\rho\sigma} R^{\mu\nu\rho\sigma} \leq \ell_P^{-4}$
3. Derive the replacement of the classical Big Bang singularity with a Planck-density coherence state
4. Resolve the black hole singularity: the interior reaches a maximum density then transitions to a regular core
5. Address the trans-Planckian problem for Hawking radiation

## Dependencies

This derivation requires:
- [Einstein Field Equations](/derivations/spacetime/einstein-equations) — classical GR with its singularity theorems
- [Holographic Entropy Bound](/derivations/holography/area-scaling) — the minimum resolution scale (Structural Postulate S1)
