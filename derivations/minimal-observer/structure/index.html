Statement

Theorem. The minimal observer — the simplest non-trivial structure satisfying Axioms 1–3 simultaneously — is a cyclic system with a two-element state space (discrete case) or a U(1)U(1)U(1) phase oscillator (continuous case), carrying exactly one independent conserved charge, with a coherence domain boundary. This structure is a Noether charge locus: the minimal realization of a U(1)U(1)U(1) symmetry with its associated conserved charge in the coherence geometry.
