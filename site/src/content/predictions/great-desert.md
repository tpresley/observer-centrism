## Prediction

No new physics between the electroweak scale ($\sim 246$ GeV) and the Planck scale ($\sim 10^{19}$ GeV). No GUT scale, no intermediate symmetry breaking, no new particles in the desert between TeV and $10^{16}$ GeV. The hierarchy of crystallization scales is complete as observed.

## Derivation Sketch

1. The observer hierarchy develops through crystallization cascades
2. Each crystallization is at a scale set by coherence coupling strength
3. The known crystallizations (electroweak, QCD) exhaust the available cascade
4. No additional cascade level exists between electroweak and Planck

## Testability

Falsifiable by future colliders discovering new particles at intermediate scales with no connection to the Standard Model's symmetry structure. This is a strong prediction.
