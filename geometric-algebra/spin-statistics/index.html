Connection to Framework Derivation

Target: Spin-Statistics (status: rigorous)

The spin-statistics derivation establishes that integer-spin observers are bosons (symmetric exchange) and half-integer-spin observers are fermions (antisymmetric exchange). The proof relies on the topological distinction between 2π2\pi2π and 4π4\pi4π rotations in the rotation group, which maps to the double cover SU(2)→SO(3)\mathrm{SU}(2) \to \mathrm{SO}(3)SU(2)→SO(3).
