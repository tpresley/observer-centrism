## Statement

**Goal.** Derive the neutrino mass mechanism from the observer loop winding structure, resolving the Dirac vs. Majorana question.

The derivation should:

1. Analyze whether neutrino winding configurations are self-conjugate (Majorana) or distinct from their conjugates (Dirac)
2. Derive the neutrino mass scale from the winding overlap with the electroweak crystallization
3. Explain the smallness of neutrino masses relative to charged leptons
4. If Majorana: derive the seesaw mechanism from the observer hierarchy level separation
5. If Dirac: explain the absence of right-handed neutrino interactions
6. Connect to the PMNS mixing matrix structure from [Flavor Mixing](/derivations/flavor/mixing-angles)

## Dependencies

This derivation requires:
- [Three Generations](/derivations/particles/three-generations) — the generation structure from division algebra triality
- [Flavor Mixing](/derivations/flavor/mixing-angles) — the PMNS matrix and mixing angle predictions

## Implications

The Dirac vs. Majorana answer has downstream consequences:
- **Majorana** → lepton number violation → leptogenesis pathway for baryogenesis
- **Dirac** → lepton number conserved → alternative baryogenesis mechanism needed
- Both cases: testable by neutrinoless double beta decay experiments (LEGEND, nEXO)
